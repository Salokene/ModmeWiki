style="color:#00ffff;">#NAMESPACE&#39;S</span><br /> <br />You may see in some scripts a #namespace. These basically designates a different name for the script than its actual filename. A #namespace goes in the preamble and looks like this:<br /> <br />[CODE=php]#namespace zombie_utility;[/CODE]<br /> <br />it&#39;s as simple as putting <span style="color:#ffcc00;">#namespace</span> followed by the name you want to give the script.<br /> <br />Note that:<br /><ul><li>You can only use one #namespace per script.
